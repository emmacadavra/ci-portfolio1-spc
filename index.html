About the Chorus

Welcome to the homepage of South Yorkshire's foremost large mixed-voice choir!

Sheffield, like many towns and cities in Yorkshire, has a long and distinguished tradition of music-making, particularly with its choruses. The Sheffield Philharmonic Chorus was officially formed alongside the Sheffield Philhamornic Society in 1935, following the opening of Sheffield City Hall in 1935, though the chorus' origins date back many decades further.
